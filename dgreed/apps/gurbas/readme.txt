A game about rebellion, escapism and alienation.
Break free from the dominion of artificial intelligence
driven by questionable goals. Use the arrows keys and
Z for control.

Most of the work was done in 48 hours, during
InfoShow GameJam '11. The theme was "do not
disturb".
