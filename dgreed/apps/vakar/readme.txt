
 vakar

A surreal story about dreams, cats and 
limitless possibilities. Done in 48 hours
during Global Game Jam '12 in Kaunas.

Control cat with arrows, z or up for jump,
r resets level.

Music is called "Cool Vibes", licensed
under Creative Commons (CC-BY) by Kevin
