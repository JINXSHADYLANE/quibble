
gija

Cooperative two-player game about connection, trust, our differences
and that hole in your chest.

Done in 48 hours during Global Game Jam 2013 in Kaunas, Lithuania.

Controls:
a, d, w, s - move, jump, give the heart away (blue person)
left, rigt, down, up - move, jump, give the heart away (red person)
r - reset level
