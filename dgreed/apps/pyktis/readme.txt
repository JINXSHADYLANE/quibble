- Lewis Carrol


A puzzle game. Designed for two players,
but can be played by one as well.

Made in 48 hours during Global Game Jam 2014, Vilnius.

Music is called "a good day for raising ships",
by The Fucked Up Beat. Licensed under CC-BY-NC-ND.

Controls:
arrows - fist player movement
w, a, s, d - second player movement
space - undo last move
esc - reset level
