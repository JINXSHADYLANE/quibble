plikledis

An attempt to do storytelling in a form of sokoban 
puzzles, based on a short story "In" by Ken Perlin.

Controls - arrows, z to undo, r to reset.

Source code can be found at code.google.com/p/quibble.
Music - "Lightless Dawn" by Kevin MacLeod, licensed 
under Creative Commons Attributiobn (CC-BY).
